About Me

Hi! My name is Roberto Reyes and I am a recent Electrical Engineering graduate with a focus on Software Engineering from The University of Texas at Austin. I'm experienced in Python, Java, C, C++, Assembly, React, Flask, HTML, and CSS. I have worked on many projects, some of which involve web development, real-time communication, data management, and algorithm design.

Some of the notable projects I have worked on include a Hardware-as-a-Service (HaaS) web application, a multi-client auction system in Java, a Spotify recommendation engine, and a dynamic programming algorithm for Atlantis food optimization. I am committed to continuous skill development and learning, and I'm passionate about creating innovative software solutions. I am eager to contribute to a team that values collaboration, growth, and excellence in software engineering. Feel free to explore my GitHub repositories and connect with me on LinkedIn to learn more about my work and interests.
